What is LIO?

LIO is a mobile app that brings powerful machine-learning tools to your phone. Built with Flutter and Google ML Kit, it helps you analyze images, recognize text, detect faces, and understand language — without sending your data to the cloud for every task.

📱

On your device

Many tools process input locally for speed and privacy, so you can work anywhere without always relying on a server.

👁️

Vision tools

Scan barcodes, recognize text, detect objects, faces, poses, and more — all from your camera or photo gallery.

💬

Language tools

Identify languages and extract useful entities from text to speed up everyday reading and writing tasks.

⭐

Built for daily use

Search tools, mark favorites, revisit recent features, and switch between light and dark mode for comfortable use day or night.
